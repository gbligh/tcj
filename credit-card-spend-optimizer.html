Not Financial Advice
This website is not financial advice and may not have up-to-date information. Please do your own reserach.
Assumptions
This chart assumes the following point valuations: Aeroplan/MR = 2.1 cents per point, Avion = 2.0 cents per point (plus 30% transfer bonus), Scene+ = 1.0 cent per point, and TD = .5 cent per point.
First Year Free
* means first year free. After your first year there is likely an annual fee. In almost all cases you can cancel the card before the second year annual fee.
Scotia Amex Groceries
The 6x Scene+ points on every $1 CAD you spend in Canada at only counts at Sobeys, Safeway, FreshCo, and Foodland.
Cash Value per M.S.R.
The 8x TD Points on every $1 CAD you spend is only eligible on purchases made on the Expedia for TD Portal.
