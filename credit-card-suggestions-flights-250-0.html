Canadian Credit Card Finder

These Cards Have the Best Bonus For You!

Points for flights • No income requirement • Low monthly spend

#1 - No Minimum Spend

RBC Avion Visa Infinite

Earn 35,000 Avion points on approval. Thats $350 in value that can be transferred 1:1 to British Airways Avios (often with a 30%-50% bonus), Westjet Dollars, or Cathay Pacific Asia Miles.

Annual fee: $120

#2 - Aeroplan Point Bonus

Amex Aeroplan

Earn 50,000 Aeroplan points on $3,000 of spend in 6 months ($500 per month). That's a minimum of $500 in cash value, but can be worth $1,000+ with strategic redemptions.

Annual fee: $120

#3 - Lower Spend Requirement

TD Aeroplan Platinum

Earn 20,000 Aeroplan points on $1,000 of spend in 3 months ($333 per month). That's a minimum of $200 in cash value, but can be worth $400+ with strategic redemptions.

Annual fee: $0*

#4 - Great Keeper Card

Amex Cobalt Card

Earn up to 30,000 Membership Rewards points (which transfer to Aeroplan 1:1) with $500 of spend each month for up to 12 months. In addition, earn up great multipliers on spend categories like 5x the points on food and drinks.

Annual fee: $156

#5 - Scene+ Points

Scotia Amex Gold

Earn 25,000 Scene+ points (cash value of $250) on $1,000 of spend in your first 3 months. Scene+ points can be used on a wide variety of redemptions, but cannot be redeemed for more than their stated value (other points like Aeroplan and Avios can)

Annual fee: $0*

#6 - Marriott Bonvoy Points

Amex Marriott Bonvoy

Earn 55,000 Marriott Bonvoy points (cash value of $495) on $1,500 of spend. These points are best used on hotels, but can be transferred to various airline partners.

Annual fee: $120

Min. Spend Requirement
When signing up for a new credit card, most will require you spend a minimum amount of money on the card to earn a sign up bonus (in points). This tool helps show which cards may have the best fit for you.
Lounge Access
These lounges typically offer complimentary food and drinks, comfortable seating, and access to charging stations and Wi-Fi. They are reserved for travelers who have a class of ticket or credit card.
Assumptions
This tool attempts to include the value of lounge visits, credits, rebates, and other bonuses in determining which credit card gives the best bonuses. In reality, the best card will depend on many more factors.
First Year Free
* means first year free. After your first year there is likely an annual fee. In almost all cases you can cancel the card before the second year annual fee.
